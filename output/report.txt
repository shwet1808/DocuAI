# Executive Summary
NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) 2026 is the national-level undergraduate medical entrance examination for India, conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). Its primary purpose is to serve as a single, uniform gateway for admission to MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, and other medical courses in all approved government, private, and deemed institutions across the country. The exam is expected to follow the established pen-and-paper (OMR-based) pattern, testing candidates on Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (Botany & Zoology) from the NCERT-based Class 11 and 12 syllabi. While the exact schedule for 2026 is not yet published, it typically follows an annual cycle with registration in early spring and the exam in May.

# Detailed Analysis

**1. Purpose and Conducting Body:**
NEET-UG is mandated by the National Medical Commission Act, 2019, to standardize medical admissions, ensure merit-based selection, and eliminate the need for multiple entrance exams. It is conducted solely by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

**2. Exam Pattern for NEET-UG (Expected for 2026):**
The pattern has remained consistent. Any official change will be notified by NTA.
*   **Mode:** Offline (Pen and Paper on OMR sheet).
*   **Duration:** 3 hours and 20 minutes (200 minutes).
*   **Number of Questions:** 200 questions (20 optional internal choices).
*   **Sections:** Physics (50 Qs), Chemistry (50 Qs), Botany (50 Qs), Zoology (50 Qs).
*   **Type of Questions:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) with four options.
*   **Marking Scheme:** +4 for each correct answer, -1 for each incorrect answer. Unattempted questions score 0.
*   **Medium:** Available in 13 languages including English, Hindi, and regional languages.
*   **Total Marks:** 720.

**3. NEET 2026 Syllabus:**
The syllabus is strictly based on the NCERT curriculum for Classes 11 and 12. The following is a consolidated overview:

*   **Physics:** Class 11: Physical World & Measurement, Kinematics, Laws of Motion, Work, Energy & Power, Motion of System of Particles & Rigid Body, Gravitation, Properties of Bulk Matter, Thermodynamics, Behaviour of Perfect Gas & Kinetic Theory, Oscillations & Waves.
    Class 12: Electrostatics, Current Electricity, Magnetic Effects of Current & Magnetism, Electromagnetic Induction & Alternating Currents, Electromagnetic Waves, Optics, Dual Nature of Matter & Radiation, Atoms & Nuclei, Electronic Devices.

*   **Chemistry:** Class 11: Some Basic Concepts of Chemistry, Structure of Atom, Classification of Elements & Periodicity in Properties, Chemical Bonding & Molecular Structure, States of Matter: Gases & Liquids, Thermodynamics, Equilibrium, Redox Reactions, Hydrogen, s-Block Elements, p-Block Elements, Organic Chemistry: Basic Principles & Techniques, Hydrocarbons, Environmental Chemistry.
    Class 12: Solid State, Solutions, Electrochemistry, Chemical Kinetics, Surface Chemistry, General Principles & Processes of Isolation of Elements, p-Block Elements, d- and f-Block Elements, Coordination Compounds, Haloalkanes & Haloarenes, Alcohols, Phenols & Ethers, Aldehydes, Ketones & Carboxylic Acids, Organic Compounds Containing Nitrogen, Biomolecules, Polymers, Chemistry in Everyday Life.

*   **Biology (Botany & Zoology):** Class 11: Diversity in Living World, Structural Organisation in Animals & Plants, Cell Structure & Function, Plant Physiology, Human Physiology.
    Class 12: Reproduction, Genetics & Evolution, Biology & Human Welfare, Biotechnology & Its Applications, Ecology & Environment.
